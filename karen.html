About Us

At Kamsikabash Farms, we are passionate about the art and science of animal rearing. For us, farming is more than just raising animals—it’s about nurturing life, ensuring sustainability, and providing healthy, quality food for families and communities.Our farm specializes in the rearing of cows, chickens, goats, and pigs, alongside the production and sales of fresh meat products. With a commitment to modern farming practices combined with traditional care, we ensure that every animal is raised in a safe, clean, and healthy environment.Our journey started with a simple vision: to rear animals with care, supply food with integrity, and build a sustainable farming system that benefits everyone. Over the years, that vision has grown into a thriving farm known for excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction.
